Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Rutz ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered running coaching application for iOS, developed by Itay Naim, based in Israel. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Rutz app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and basic profile details you provide when creating an account.
  • Garmin Activity Data: When you connect your Garmin account, we retrieve workout and activity data including runs, heart rate, cadence, ground contact time, vertical oscillation, stride length, training effect, VO2 max estimates, and other metrics available through the Garmin Connect API.
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used, training plans viewed, and coaching feedback received.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility.

2. How We Collect Your Data

We collect data through:

  • Direct input: Information you provide when creating your account or setting up your profile.
  • Garmin Connect API: Activity and health data retrieved with your explicit consent when you connect your Garmin account. You must authorize this connection, and you can revoke it at any time.
  • Automatic collection: Basic app usage and device data collected during normal use of the app.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Generating personalized AI-powered training plans and coaching recommendations.
  • Analyzing your running performance, fitness trends, and recovery patterns.
  • Improving the accuracy and quality of coaching insights over time.
  • Providing run scoring, readiness assessments, and race preparation guidance.
  • App maintenance, troubleshooting, and improving the user experience.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Rutz:

  • Garmin Connect API: To retrieve your activity and training data with your permission.
  • AI Processing (OpenAI / Anthropic): Your activity data may be sent to AI language model APIs for the sole purpose of generating personalized coaching insights. These services process data in real time and do not permanently store your personal data beyond what is needed to complete the request.
  • Cloud Hosting (AWS / Supabase): For secure data storage and app infrastructure.
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us at itaynaim@gmail.com. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove your data from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data and account.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time, including disconnecting your Garmin account.
  • Export your data in a portable format upon request.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at itaynaim@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Rutz is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Legal Compliance

This policy is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for users in the European Union.
  • Israeli Privacy Protection Law, 5741-1981 and related regulations.
  • Apple App Store Guidelines regarding data collection and privacy.
  • Garmin Connect Developer Program requirements for handling user activity data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify you through the app if the changes are significant. Continued use of Rutz after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
